**Maker Thursday** is a weekly, community-driven gathering at **TinkerSpace**, bringing together makers, tinkerers, students, and curious minds to connect, share, and build.

Each session may have a planned topic, project sharing, or demo or it may evolve organically based on the interests of the people who show up. The format stays flexible to encourage open discussion, hands-on making, and spontaneous collaboration.

Whether you are into electronics, hardware hacking, software, fabrication, or just starting out, Maker Thursday is an open space to learn, experiment, and build together.

---

## Contributors & Community

Maker Thursday is run by the community, for the community.
It thrives on the contributions of people who share knowledge, ideas, and time.

### Contributors
- Session hosts and speakers
- Project and demo presenters
- Workshop facilitators

### Community Members
- Mentors
- Regular participants
- First-time attendees

> Interested in contributing or hosting a session?
> Reach out to the organizers or propose a topic during the meetup.
